Mina Moon



Nomz

In Collaboration with Chelsea Hwang

Nomz is a food themed app that provides users with quick and delicious recipes conveniently made with the present ingredients in their own fridge. It's targeted towards people with tight grocery budgets or busy schedules. With Nomz, you'll learn about new recipes that require minimal ingredients while keeping your wallet happy and deadlines met.




The App


We decided on the name "Nomz" because it adorably resembles and exaggerates the sound of someone nibbling on food. After brainstorming about how to incorporate food into a simple logo, we decided on a fried egg on a fry pan due to the simplicity and consistency of round shapes.
