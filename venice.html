Romance in Venice

What comes up in your head when you think of the word romantic?




The Design


I don't know about you, but I thought of the city of romance, Venice. For years and years, Venice has always been a favourite place to visit for romantic vacations and honeymoons. Who can blame them - Its breathtaking scenery and colours makes it feel as if you're in a dream. This expressive typography poster captures that same energy. I used the letterforms of the word "Romance" to recapture Venice's beautiful buildings and water on a bright summery day.
