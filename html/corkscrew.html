Corkscrew

Corkscrew is a conceptual app that allows all users to explore new types of wine. If you're tired of the typical wine apps that are only user friendly to wine enthusiasts, this is the app for you. Corkscrew will be your personal assistant in finding the perfect wine, suited perfectly to your style and tastes.




The Design


Corkscrew is made with original illustrations that capture an elegant and edgy feel. Corkscrew's name was chosen because it's simple and implies wine right away. We designed several logos which cleverly combined a corkscrew and wine glass. In the end, the corkscrew wrapped around the wine bottle was chosen.








The Problem


Cosmetics are often marketed towards specific genders. By utilizing an androgynous design, this package can appeal to both men and women. It minimizes gender distinctions, focusing on simplicity and emphasizing the theme of cleanliness.
