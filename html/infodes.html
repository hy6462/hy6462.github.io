Rich Man's Justice

Consisted of an infographic and an accompanying interactive tablet infograph, this project critiques the flawed law system that relies too heavily on money.

The more you can spend, the more lawyers you can hire, the more “experts” you can buy to tell the jury things that will bewilder them, the more private investigators you can hire to dig up dirt on your legal opponents. But if you are short of cash, watch out. The legal system is not a place where the poor are well accommodated.

The design is intended to be eye catching and powerful. Using bright colours and line illustrations emphasized this effect.
