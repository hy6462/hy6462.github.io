Moon

Moon is a conceptual cosmetics brand focused on cleanliness and minimal design.



The Problem


Cosmetics are often marketed towards specific genders.




The Solution


By utilizing an androgynous design, this package can appeal to both men and women. It minimizes gender distinctions, focusing on simplicity and emphasizing the theme of cleanliness. Moon is packaged into 3 simple steps, minimizing the process, but contains the products of an effective night skincare routine. The waves in the front of the box gives an interesting touch and depth to the box, making it eye catching even if the overall design is minimal. While the box is pulled out of the cover, an animation of the phases of the moon are shown, emphasizing the overall brand and adding an interesting touch to it. The labels that wrap around each product are straight forward, eliminating any extra information that anyone could be confused about.
