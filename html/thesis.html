Fearless App

In contrast to being shy, those with social anxiety find that it prevents them to complete everyday tasks.

Starting off as my thesis project from University, focusing on social anxiety, this new and improved app focuses on the broader picture of mental health issues in the University students.
Students seldom look for counselling services due to schedule and time restraints, worries about people finding out about their conditions, or a lack of information and resources to see a counselor. Sometimes students have to wait several weeks, or even months to see a counselor.

This app design encourages student to reach out to their counselors via mobile.

Login

Access the school's counseling services by searching for, selecting, then connecting your student account to the Fearless app.

Fill Out Forms

Before entering counseling, students are usually asked to fill out forms of consent and areas of need. Filling these out once through mobile (and being able to edit it later) quickens the process.

Select Your Ideal Date and Time

Make an appointment based on your availbility. Times and dates that are not available are crossed out. You can either choose to have a meeting via text, phone or in person.

Select an Available Counselor

The app generates compaitable times with a counselor.

Review Your Appointments

View a list of your appointments.

Chat With Your Counselor.

An example of a text appointment with a counselor

Journal Feature

Track your daily mood, allowing you to analyze how effective the counseling sessions are.
