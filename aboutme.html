About Me

Hi! I'm a strong listener with a big heart who loves to solve problems through empathy. Throughout my design studies, I have developed a skill of finding a midway ground between my passion for fine arts and love for design in my work. I strive to make things colourful and expressive, but also practical and useable. I dabble in interaction design, and recently have found a new interest for motion graphics. I previously interned at Helios Design Labs, Entro Communications, and freelanced for a startup called Knowledgehook.

I'm a senior at YSDN, set to graduate this April!
